Segway'n USA


Documenting my trip around the US on my very own Segway!


June 2, 2012


y first day of the trip! I can’t believe I finally got everything packed and ready to go. Because I’m on a Segway, I wasn’t able to bring a whole lot with me:

  1. Cell phone
  2. IPod
  3. Digital camera
  4. Protien bar

Just the essentials. As Lao Tzu would have said, A journey of a thousand miles begins with one Segway.


July 14, 2012

I definitely won’t be passing any cars.
